Riverside Office Renovation - Sample Contract Clauses

Clause 8.2 - Weather Delay
The Contractor may request a schedule extension for abnormal weather conditions that directly impact critical path work. The Contractor must provide written notice within seven calendar days of the weather event and include supporting evidence.

Clause 9.1 - Change Orders
Changes to the scope, schedule, or contract sum must be documented through a written change order approved by the Owner before affected work proceeds, unless emergency conditions require immediate action.

Clause 10.4 - Notice Requirement
The Contractor must provide timely written notice for any condition that may impact cost or schedule. Failure to provide notice may limit recovery.

Clause 12.3 - Owner-Caused Delay
If the Owner fails to provide required decisions, materials, approvals, or site access, the Contractor may request an equitable adjustment to time and cost.

Clause 14.2 - Non-Excusable Delay
Delays caused by Contractor labor shortages, poor planning, or failure to coordinate subcontractors are not excusable unless otherwise approved in writing by the Owner.
